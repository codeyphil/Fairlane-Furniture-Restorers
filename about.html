About FFR

Through care and attention to detail, we have been setting the industry standard for how to care for and restore your damaged contents for over 50 years. While the times have certainly changed, our vision and our drive to serve you and bring your life back to how it should be remains constant.

What we do...


Since 1958, our services have evolved to include complete furniture and contents restoration, customized for each specific loss. These losses could result from fire, water, smoke, storm and transit damage. We provide complete contents pack outs and removals, as well as onsite and in plant cleaning. Specialty services include: custom refinishing & reupholstering.

We service fourteen counties in Lower Michigan - from Lapeer to Monroe, from St. Clair to Jackson and more. At Fairlane Furniture Restorers, our blend of skills, judgment and personal contact ensures your complete satisfaction.

Who we are...


Fairlane Furniture Restorers had it's beginnings in Dearborn, MI. Up until 1958, the company was cleaning and selling carpet. This was until multiple insurance companies approached the company to see if Fairlane had an interest in a new field that would be titled "insurance restoration". The insurance companies were looking for businesses to specialize in cleaning home contents. Ultimately, that is the direction Fairlane Furniture Restorers would decide to go. It essentially served as one of the pioneers in the advent and development of the contents restoration aspect of the insurance industry.

It wasn't until years later, in 2002, that the Johnsons decided to move Fairlane to Taylor, Michigan. Growing up in Taylor, and having invested in the Downriver community, Ken Johnson, company owner, describes the move as a decision to "bring the company back home". The move to Taylor was close to the heart of it's owner, as he was raised in the very same town.

Fairlane Furniture Restorers has been located in Taylor since then and continues to deliver quality services and exceptional customer care by giving you the very best in contents restoration!
