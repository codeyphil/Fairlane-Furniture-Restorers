Fairlane Furniture Restorers

We are a family-owned business with over 50 years of experience as a liaison between the insurer and the insured. We are experts in insurance contents restoration resulting from fire, water, smoke, storm and transit damage.

Contents Inventory & Packouts - Restoration - Cleanup & Disposal of Total Loss Contents

After a loss occurs, many of the affected contents must be removed from the property in order to compliment repairs to the structure of the property. This may entail full packouts, restoration of the contents, cleanup and disposal of the contents, or in the best case scenario, manipulation of the contents that will be stored onsite. Regardless of your situation, we feel confident that we can effectively assist you in getting back on your feet!
