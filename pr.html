Spelling Out Our Main Services

Fairlane Furniture Restorers provides complete service to a full realm of items:


  • Upholstered (or soft) furniture including lamp shades, box springs and mattresses.
  • Wood (or hard) furniture
  • All types of miscellaneous items

Cartage Services:


  • Wrapping, packing, removal, storage (until the house is complete) and delivery of the entirety of the contents.

  • After delivery, if desired, Fairlane will put all contents back into place.

Cleaning, Repair and Restoration:

  • Test cleaning - a special service that we provide to determine the extent of the damage to the contents.
  • Onsite and in plant cleaning (dry and/or wet).
  • Deodorizing, polishing, repairing, restoring, reupholstering and refinishing.

Inventory, Cleanup & Disposal of Total Loss Contents

  • FFR will digitally inventory (including photograph) all total loss contents. We will generate the finalized inventory in Excel spreadsheet format, and will submit it to the insurance carrier and/or the policyholder to allow for continued aging and replacement cost pricing.
  • In certain circumstances, FFR can cleanup and dispose of the total loss contents once the finalized inventory has been submitted.
